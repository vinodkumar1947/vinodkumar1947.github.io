About

Built around real systems, not demos

I work best where hardware, software, and operational constraints intersect: production firmware, data movement from device to cloud, lab validation, and the practical details that make connected products stable in the field.

My background covers embedded systems, IoT platforms, backend services, and software engineering across product and consulting environments. I have spent more than a decade solving for reliability under real constraints: battery life, weak connectivity, intermittent power, testability, and the cost of bad field behavior.

The work is rarely just firmware or just cloud. It is usually the whole path: sensor input, edge behavior, transport, payload design, persistence, monitoring, and the test infrastructure that proves the system before it ships.

I also mentor engineers and support teams that need sharper development workflows, better debugging discipline, and a cleaner path from prototype to production.
