About

Built around real systems, not demos

I work across the full lifecycle of connected products: device firmware, connectivity, telemetry, backend tooling, and validation, with a focus on systems that need to work outside the lab.

My background spans embedded systems, IoT platforms, software engineering, and consulting delivery. I work across firmware, connectivity, telemetry, backend tooling, and validation to help teams build systems that are production-ready rather than just functional in a demo environment.

The problems I solve best are the practical ones: unstable field behavior, weak connectivity, power constraints, limited observability, and slow debugging cycles. I help turn prototypes into dependable systems by improving architecture, validation, and operational clarity.

Validation Robot PyTest

Hardware-in-the-Loop Validation Workflow

Result: faster debugging cycles

Problem: Firmware regressions were too expensive to discover late, and reproducing failures manually was slow and inconsistent.

Approach: Built automated validation flows using HIL-style setups, test harnesses, and repeatable checks to expose regressions earlier in the cycle.

Outcome: Faster debugging, better release discipline, and reduced risk before deployment or pilot stages.

Stack: PyTest, Robot Framework, Python, HIL, CI
